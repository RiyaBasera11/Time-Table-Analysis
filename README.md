## 📌 Project Overview
This dashboard helps track and visualize study behavior across multiple subjects. It provides insights into total study hours, productivity trends, and efficiency distribution to help improve time management and learning outcomes.

---

## 🔍 Key Features
- **Overall KPIs**: Total Study Hours, Average Study per Day, Total Subjects Covered  
- **Subject-wise Analysis**: Comparison of study hours and productivity across Math, Science, Data Science, Programming, Revision, and English  
- **Performance Trends**: Study pattern analysis over time (simulated dataset)  
- **Weekly Insights**: Day-wise study distribution with weekday vs weekend comparison  
- **Efficiency Analysis**: Relationship between study hours and productivity score  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Saturday shows the highest overall study engagement  
- Math accounts for the highest total study time  
- Science demonstrates strong productivity efficiency with relatively lower hours  
- Weekends contribute a significant portion of total study time  
- Overall productivity target achievement is approximately 93%

## 📁 Dataset Information
The dataset (`Timetable_Data`) includes:
- Date, Day, Day Type  
- Subject-wise study hours  
- Productivity score  
- Sleep hours & mobile usage  
- Target productivity metrics  

> Note: This is a simulated dataset created for learning and visualization purposes.
